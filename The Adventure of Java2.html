The Adventure of Java

Batik String Art

This string art is made with a distinctive batik motif concept. The motifs used are inspired by traditional batik patterns, which have deep cultural and philosophical values. Batik is an Indonesian cultural heritage that is rich in meaning. With the string art technique, batik motifs can be visualized in a more modern and unique form.

The Identity of Java

A poster depicting the characteristics of the Java region, especially Central Java, such as traditional houses, typical foods, monuments, etc. which are combined in the form of a poster.

Selat of Solo

"Selat of Solo" is not just a replica of food, but also a form of respect for the diversity of Indonesian cuisine in the world of art. typical food of Solo City, in the form of clay art. Made with details that depict the main components of this dish. Selat Solo is a dish that reflects the fusion of Javanese and European cultures. By representing it in the form of art, this work immortalizes one of the typical culinary arts of the archipelago.

Joglo House

This work is a simple yet meaningful representation of the traditional Joglo house which until now is still a symbol of Javanese cultural identity. The Joglo house is a symbol of traditional Javanese culture and architecture. The Joglo traditional house is known as the center of Javanese family life, a place where many generations gather under one roof. By creating this miniature, this work becomes a form of appreciation for the heritage of our ancestors.
