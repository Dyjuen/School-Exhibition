The Adventure of Papua

he Bag of Culture

The Bag of Culture is inspired by the noken bag, a bag commonly used by traditional Papuan people, which is also one of the natural wealth and traditions in Papua. This bag is made by knitting or crocheting manually and usually uses materials that come from nature. This bag has a meaning as a symbol of Papuan identity and culture, the ability and skills of the community, patience and perseverance, as well as honor and wealth.

The White Gold of Papua

The White Gold of Papua is a White Shell Choker Necklace that comes from Papua. This Papua White Shell Necklace is often worn by Papuans in traditional events and also as jewelry when they wear traditional clothes. This shell depicts the luxury and wealth that is owned.

The Wonderful of Papua

The Wonderful of Papua is a screen-printed t-shirt with a design themed on the natural beauty of Papua. There is a print of a turquoise water landscape with small rocky islands, reflecting the exotic charm of Papua, more precisely in Raja Ampat. The writing "WONDERFUL OF PAPUA" also emphasizes its natural beauty, making this t-shirt a souvenir or appreciation of the beauty of Papua.

The Hope From Papua

The Hope From Papua is a dream catcher inspired by the natural beauty of Papua, which is often referred to as the hidden pearl of Indonesia. With a combination of natural materials such as feathers, beads, and threads, this dream catcher symbolizes purity, beauty, and peace. Every detail reflects the richness of Papua's culture and natural charm, making it more than just a decoration, but also a symbol of serenity and good luck.

The House of Mother Nature

The House of Mother Nature is a string art of Rumah Honai because Rumah Honai is a symbol of culture and identity of the Dani tribe which is quite famous in Papua. This house also reflects local intelligence in utilizing existing natural materials and shows the way of life of the Papuan people who are close to nature.
