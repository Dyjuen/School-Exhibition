The Adventure of Sulawesi

Rooster of Hasannudin

The painting titled "Sides of Kalimantan" carries the meaning that Kalimantan can create extraordinary beauty through its two distinct aspects. The first is through the culture they have inherited over generations, with a wide variety of differences. The second is through the economy and technology that continue to develop rapidly with the progression of time. These two aspects complement each other to create a more diverse, extraordinary, and beautiful harmony. This teaches us that culture and technology can create a better world if applied in balance.

The Beauty of Sombori

Pop Art 3D is an artwork made primarily from paper. We create a 3D effect by stacking layers of paper, making it thicker and giving it a sense of depth. The Paperclip artwork titled "Kalimantan, the Lungs of Indonesia" tells the story of Kalimantan's rich forests and trees, which serve as a vital source of oxygen for human life.
