The Adventure of Banjarmasin

“The Wonders of Banjarmasin” Poster

The Wonders of Banjarmasin is a poster that contains the picture of tourist attractions or iconic places from the city of Banjarmasin, such as Menara Pandang, Taman Kelayan, Patung Bekantan and Pasar terapung.

The "Flower" of Banjarmasin

The "Flower" of Banjarmasin is an epithet for floating market miniature. In this miniature, it shows a buying and selling activity on a "jukung" or traditional boat on the river. In this miniature, a small interaction is shown between buyers and sellers who are selling/purchasing goods that are local food products.

“The House of the Nobles” in Banjarmasin

“The House of the Nobles” in Banjarmasin is a "Bubungan Tinggi" house which is a traditional house from South Kalimantan. As the name implies, this house is higher than the usual houses, because the area around the house is filled with swamps and rivers and also this house was a house for the nobles in the past. In the yard of the house, there is a typical South Kalimantan game, namely "Balogo" which is usually played by children to adults in South Kalimantan.
