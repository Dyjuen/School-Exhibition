The Adventure of Java

Sangkara Java: Ijen Terra

An adventure to enter deep into the tropical forest of Java, where various flora grow and have been living, like endemic plants and exotic trees. With green strokes into light and dark, there is a three-dimensional effect as if inviting the audience on a journey into the wild nature of Java. The details, like branches and shadows, can symbolize the balance of the ecosystem.

Sangkara Java: Southern Sea

The Southern Sea, also known as the Indian Ocean, holds great significance in Javanese culture and mythology. This artwork captures the raw power and beauty of the Southern Sea, which has long been associated with Nyai Roro Kidul, the mythical Queen of the Southern Sea in Javanese folklore.

❮ ❯

Golden Ink of Wayang: Gatotkaca's Might

Gatotkaca is one of the most powerful and beloved characters in Javanese wayang (shadow puppet) tradition. Known for his extraordinary strength and ability to fly, he represents courage, loyalty, and protection. This artwork celebrates his legendary might through traditional Javanese artistic elements combined with modern design techniques.

❮ ❯

Golden Ink of Wayang: Madri's Loyalty & The Mountain

This piece depicts Madri, a character from the Mahabharata whose story exemplifies unwavering loyalty and sacrifice. The mountain backdrop represents Mount Merapi, one of Java's most sacred volcanoes, creating a powerful connection between Javanese mythology and its natural landscape.
