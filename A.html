"Adventure of Indonesia"

Welcome to the 9A Class Exhibition: Indonesian Culture!

This exhibition is a collective effort by Class 9A to celebrate the rich and diverse cultural heritage of Indonesia. Through art, tradition, and creativity, we bring you a glimpse of Indonesia’s beauty across Six iconic islands—Java, Nusa Tenggara, Papua, Sulawesi, Sumatra, and Kalimantan.

Each island carries its own unique traditions, from mesmerizing dances and intricate textiles to breathtaking artworks and historical artifacts. Our exhibition is a tribute to the million priceless cultures that make Indonesia extraordinary.

Step into our world and immerse yourself in the wonders of Indonesian heritage. We hope you enjoy the journey!
